Here at Bar Maroc, cocktail creation is our passion, you will always find an extensive list of drinks for you to enjoy. Ciders

Cider; the drink itself is so versatile, so we decided to reflect that within our cider menu. We have a few ciders on tap - Thatchers Gold & Strongbow, Seasonal Cider taps, and a large range of bottles to suit every preference. We cover all flavours- Pear, Apple and Fruity ciders all while still providing a few gluten free cider options.
