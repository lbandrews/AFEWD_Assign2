Who are we?

We are Bar Maroc, a quaint cocktail bar located at the edge of the town centre in Huddersfield.

Although we identify as a bar, we also serve some of Huddersfield's best Vegetarian and Vegan pizzas, not only that, we are purveyors of some amazing accoutrements and cracking crêpes!
